Our offers

01

Service Operations Review

We review all, or specific, commercial service areas such as catering and accommodation. Our client receives a report outlining the strengths and weaknesses of the current offering, together with recommendations for future delivery to optimize financial performance and a great experience for users of the services

02

Expectations Survey

Through a comprehensive series of individual and group interviews we establish the expectations of the university’s senior management towards commercial services, gaining an understanding of the Estates Strategy. The meetings with management, staff and students are tailored to deliver a deep understanding of the attitudes and expectations of users towards their services. A set of recommendations is provided to help guide the university’s commercial team to achieve future success

03

Consumer Insight Programme

This programme offers a deeper insight into the needs and expectations of all stakeholders and service users, using a series of interviews to gain a comprehensive understanding of the requirements for catering or accommodation services. The subsequent report can assist in driving long term planning whilst providing a clear vision of the present services. For maximum effect the programme should be repeated towards the end of the Academic year and again in the following year

Each of our programmes are tailored to the specific needs of the individual institution
