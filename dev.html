Our offers

01

Service Operations Review

We review all, or specific, commercial service areas such as catering and accommodation. Our client receives a report outlining the strengths and weaknesses of the current offering, together with recommendations for future delivery to optimize financial performance and a great experience for users of the services

02

Expectations Survey

Through a comprehensive series of individual and group interviews we establish the expectations of the university’s senior management towards commercial services, whilst gaining an understanding of the Estates Strategy. A range of meetings follows with management, staff and students, tailored to secure a deep understanding of the attitudes and expectations of users towards their services. A set of recommendations is provided to help guide the university’s commercial team to achieve future success

03

Consumer Insight Programme

This programme offers a deeper insight into the needs and expectations of all stakeholders and service users, using a series of interviews to gain a comprehensive understanding of the requirements for catering or accommodation, or other student facing services. The subsequent report will assist in driving long term planning whilst providing a clear vision of the present services. For maximum effect the programme should be repeated towards the end of the Academic year and again in the following year

About Us

Weston Taylor was created in 2016 by Peter Taylor, who has over twenty years of experience in the Higher Eduction sector. Having created the Universities Division for Sodexo, Peter developed many initiatives devised to improve the quality of life for students, including the highly regarded Sodexo University Lifestyle Survey.

In 2012 Peter developed Student Living by Sodexo, which moved further into providing a comprehensive range of student services, and demanded enhanced understanding of social and welfare considerations.

FAQs

How are the programmes priced?

We offer fixed price packages based on a day rate. This allows clients to decide on the extent and scope of the work, whilst also being provided with a guaranteed cost. Using a day rate method allows for additional work to be undertaken if desired. Each programme takes between nine and fifteen days to complete.

How flexible will the research be?

The question content, as well as the size of samples to be interviewed is agreed with our clients. As well as some generic questions, we include specific subjects that have particular relevance to individual institutions.

Who determines the questions to be asked?

The questions are agreed with our nominated client team before being put to individuals and groups. If required, the questions can be modified or added to in order to ensure all relevant views are collected.

How confidential is the information that is gathered?

All the information, including the detail of the questions, is owned by the client, and will not be shared with other institutions or used generically, unless with the express permission of the client.
